   132 ## Carousel / Horizontal Paging
   133 
   134 Carousels (image sliders, onboarding, step wizards) break on react-native-web in three specific ways — follow all three rules:
   135 
   136 1. Give each horizontal `FlatList` / `ScrollView` item a **fixed width** (e.g. `SCREEN_WIDTH` from `useWindowDimensions`). NEVER add a main-axis `flex-1` to the item — on rn-web that divides one screen across all items so paging never advances.
   137 2. Make `currentIndex` **state** the source of truth: a Next button calls `setCurrentIndex`, and `scrollToIndex` is only for visual sync. NEVER drive step state purely off scroll position.
   138 3. In `onViewableItemsChanged`, only update the index when `viewableItems.length === 1` — rn-web reports ALL items visible at once, which otherwise pins `index` at `0`.
